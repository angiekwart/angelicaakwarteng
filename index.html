Knowing Angelica

Angelica Adjei-Kwarteng is a professional data analyst/ scientist with three years professional experience in using data analytics techniques for customer, operational and administrative support. She enjoys working with business stakeholders to help support strategy using research and statistical models for business.

Her professional journey began in 2017 as an insurance underwriter, where she used data to assess client risk—an experience that sparked her interest in data-driven decision-making. This early exposure to working with data led her to explore the field of data management in 2019, during which she learned her first programming language, Java, alongside SQL and Oracle databases. The challenges and opportunities she encountered while working with databases challenged her to continuous learning on the path of computer science and in 2022, she formally transitioned into data science, beginning her journey with Python for data science.

Angelica eventually became a self-taught Python developer who built web applications on side-projects and have been contributing to backend projects since 2022 while pursuing her career aspirations in the industry. She also mentored students and other professionals in python programming till she solely focused on data analytics and data science in 2024. In 2024, she became a data analyst by profession and also obtained a certification in data analytics. She supports strategy at operational level and implements them based on research and statistical models in business. Angelica is driven, curious, resilient and leverages data science and AI to make a more effective impact. She cannot wait to join a team whose mission is to impact the lives of others using data and technology.
